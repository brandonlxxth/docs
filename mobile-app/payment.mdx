## Overview

The mobile app supports multiple payment methods for checkout and account top-ups. Payment is configured in the Management Portal under **Configuration > Terminal** (Payment options tab).

## Payment methods

At checkout, members can pay with:

### Saved cards
- Previously saved credit/debit cards
- Quick checkout with saved payment details
- CVV may be required for security
- Manage saved cards in Settings

### New card
- Enter card details at checkout
- Option to save card for future use
- Supports all major card types (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Card tokenization for security

### Account credit
- Pay using prepaid account balance
- Balance must be sufficient for order total
- Can be combined with partial card payment
- View balance in Home tab

### Cost centre
- Enter cost centre code at checkout
- Charge purchase to department or project
- Cost centre must be assigned to member
- May require approval code

### Platform pay
- **Apple Pay** (iOS) - Use cards saved in Apple Wallet
- **Google Pay** (Android) - Use cards saved in Google Pay
- Touch ID / Face ID for authorization
- One-tap checkout

## Payment provider

The app uses Stripe as the payment provider, configured in the Management Portal:

### Stripe
Payment provider with support for:
- Card payments (credit/debit)
- Platform pay (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- 3D Secure authentication
- Card tokenization
- PCI compliance

Stripe is configured per outlet in **Configuration > Terminal > Payment options tab**.

## Card management

Members can manage saved payment cards:

### View cards
- See all saved cards
- Card type and last 4 digits
- Expiry date
- Default card indicator

### Save new card
- Add card at checkout (select "Save card for future use")
- Or add from Settings > Security
- Card details tokenized and stored securely
- Never store full card numbers

### Delete card
- Remove saved card from account
- Cannot delete if it's the only payment method with active subscriptions
- Deletion is immediate

### Set default card
- Choose preferred card for quick checkout
- Default card is pre-selected at checkout
- Can be changed at any time

Card data is stored securely by Stripe, not on Kappture servers or in the app.

## Account top-up

Members can add credit to their account balance:

### Top-up process
1. Go to Home tab
2. Tap account to view details
3. Tap "Top up"
4. Enter amount to add
5. Select payment method (card or platform pay)
6. Confirm and pay

### Top-up limits
Account types can have top-up limits configured in **Configuration > Member > Account**:
- Minimum top-up amount
- Maximum top-up amount
- Maximum account balance

### Top-up transaction
After successful top-up:
- Amount is added to account balance
- Transaction appears in account history
- Receipt is available in app
- Email confirmation (if enabled)

## Payment security

### PCI compliance
- Card data is tokenized by payment provider
- App never stores full card numbers
- PCI DSS compliant payment processing
- Secure HTTPS communication

### 3D Secure
- Additional authentication for card payments
- Required by some card issuers
- Redirects to bank for verification
- Supports 3D Secure 2.0

### Encryption
- All payment data encrypted in transit
- TLS 1.2+ for API communication
- Secure token storage

## Payment at checkout

### Payment flow
1. Review order and total
2. Select payment method
3. Apply vouchers or discounts
4. Confirm payment details
5. Submit order
6. Payment processed
7. Order confirmation

### Split payment
Some outlets allow splitting payment between:
- Account credit + card payment
- Multiple cards
- Cost centre + card payment

Split payment availability depends on outlet terminal configuration.

### Payment failure

If payment fails:
- Error message displayed
- Reason for failure (if provided)
- Option to try again or use different payment method
- Order is not placed

Common failure reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Card declined by issuer
- 3D Secure authentication failed
- Network error
- Expired card

## Refunds

Refunds for cancelled or modified orders:
- Processed automatically by payment provider
- Return to original payment method
- Appear in account history
- May take 3-5 business days for card refunds
- Instant for account credit refunds

Refunds are initiated from the Management Portal by administrators.

## Configuration in Management Portal

| Payment feature | Management Portal location |
|----------------|---------------------------|
| Payment provider (Stripe) | **Configuration > Terminal** (Payment options tab on ordering terminals) |
| Account types | **Configuration > Member > Account** |
| Cost centres | **Configuration > Member > Cost Centre** |
| Refund processing | **Ordering > Dashboard** (order details) |
