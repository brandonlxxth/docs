## What is the Kappture mobile app?

The Kappture mobile app is a customer-facing iOS and Android application that allows members to order food and beverages, manage loyalty programs, view rewards, check account balances, and track orders in real time.

The app connects to your Kappture system configured in the Management Portal and provides a branded mobile experience for your customers.

## Key features

### Ordering
- Browse menus and products
- Add items to cart with modifiers and options
- Select collection/delivery time slots
- Choose tables for dine-in
- Scan barcodes for quick ordering (Scan & Go)
- Track preparation progress at preparation points
- View order status in real time

### Payment
- Pay with credit/debit cards via Stripe
- Save cards for faster checkout
- Top up account balance
- Use account credit for purchases
- Apply cost centre codes
- Platform pay support (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

### Loyalty & rewards
- View and redeem vouchers
- Track loyalty points
- Browse available rewards
- View point earning strategies
- Apply vouchers at checkout

### Account management
- View account balance and transaction history
- Manage payment cards
- View and use cost centres
- Track order history
- Check current order status
- Update profile and preferences

### Communication
- Read news and announcements (Board)
- Receive push notifications for orders
- Get allergen information
- View promotions and campaigns

## How it works

1. **Member signs up** - Users create an account with email/password or sign in with existing credentials
2. **Browse & order** - Members browse products, add to cart, and checkout
3. **Payment** - Pay with saved cards, account credit, or cost centre
4. **Track order** - View real-time preparation status
5. **Collect** - Pick up order when ready or have it delivered to table

## Configuration in Management Portal

The mobile app displays content and features configured in the Management Portal:

| App feature | Management Portal location |
|-------------|---------------------------|
| Products & menus | **Configuration > Management > Products**, **Menus**, **Self-Service Menu** |
| Pricing | **Configuration > Management > Price Bands**, **Product Prices** |
| Outlets & sessions | **Configuration > Sites** (outlets), **Management > Sessions** |
| Loyalty programs | **Configuration > Incentives** (Loyalty, Rewards, Vouchers, Games) |
| Account types | **Configuration > Member > Account** |
| Cost centres | **Configuration > Member > Cost Centre** |
| Member groups | **Configuration > Member > Member Group** |
| Board (news) | **Configuration > Mobile App > Board** |
| Payment options | **Configuration > Terminal** (Payment options tab on ordering terminals) |
| Allergens | **Configuration > Management > Allergens** |
| Preparation points | **Configuration > Sites** (preparation points) |
| Resources (images) | **Configuration > Management > Resources** |

## App structure

The app uses a tab-based navigation with four main sections:

### Home tab
- Current order status
- Board (news feed)
- Accounts
- Cost centres
- Vouchers
- Points (loyalty)
- Rewards

### Scan tab
- QR code scanner for quick ordering
- Barcode scanner for Scan & Go

### Preparation Points tab
- Real-time order tracking
- Preparation status by station
- Estimated completion times

### Settings tab
- Profile management
- Security settings
- Order history
- Order status
- Notifications
- Appearance (theme)
- Legal information

## Requirements

- iOS 13.0 or later / Android 5.0 or later
- Active internet connection
- Valid member account in Kappture system
- Outlets configured with ordering terminals in Management Portal
