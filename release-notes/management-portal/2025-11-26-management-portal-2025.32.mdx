This release introduces Order to Par Level functionality for efficient stock ordering and restores "All" and "None" buttons for outlet selection in Report Options. Bug fixes address product reference searching in Stock Orders, Revenue Event outlet permissions, Stock Validator help text, Count Sheet information display, Account Top Up duplicate submissions, receipt signature lines, Pay at Table QR code validation, and Stock Movement maximum quantity enforcement.
