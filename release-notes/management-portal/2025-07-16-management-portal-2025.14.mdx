This release introduces important enhancements, including Portugal Fiscalization configuration for products and terminals, new reporting features such as Stock to Cash Reports, and improvements to stock management terminology. Additionally, critical bug fixes have addressed issues related to Vision Asset loading, Receipt Portal QR code audits, order journal calculations, CSV exports, permission errors, and various UI and data-handling improvements.
