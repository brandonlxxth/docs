## Overview

The Management Portal navigation menu is organised into several main sections. The pages you can access are determined by your user permissions and the modules your organisation has enabled.

<Warning>
Some sections require specific modules that are charged separately. These are clearly marked throughout the documentation.
</Warning>

## Module Requirements

The following sections require specific modules that are charged separately:

- **Stock Section**: Requires the **Stock Module**
- **Ordering Section**: Requires the **Ordering Module** or **Click & Collect Module**
- **Cash Management Section**: Requires the **Revenue Module**
- **Parent Pay Section**: Requires the **Parent Pay Module**
- **Pay at Table**: Requires the **Pay at Table Module** (within Configuration)
- **Receipt Portal**: Requires the **Receipt Portal Module** (within Configuration)

<Info>
When a section is missing from your navigation menu, the required module is not enabled for your organization. Contact your administrator or [Kappture Sales](/contact) to enable additional modules.
</Info>

## Quick Navigation Tips

### Using the Search

Press `Ctrl+/` (or `Cmd+/` on Mac) to open the global search. Type the name of any page to quickly navigate to it.

### Menu Organization

- Menu items are organized into collapsible sections
- Click on section headers to expand or collapse subsections
- The menu automatically highlights the current page you're viewing

### Permissions

- You'll only see menu items for features you have permission to access
- If you need access to a feature, contact your administrator
- Menu items are hidden when you lack permission for them or when the related module is not enabled
