## Overview

The Product Management section lets you maintain your product catalogue, configure pricing, organise products into groups, and set up product restrictions. This is where you define what products are available, how much they cost, and where they can be sold.

## Managing Your Product Catalog

### Viewing Products

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Products">
  Click "Products" in the main navigation menu to access the product catalog.
</Step>

<Step title="Browse Products">
  Use the **search box at the top** to find products. Type your search (e.g., product name or code) and results will appear below.
</Step>

<Step title="View Product Details">
  Click on any product to see detailed information including:
  - Product description
  - Pricing information
  - Allergen information
  - Stock levels (when the product has stock)
  - Where the product is available
</Step>
</Steps>

### Creating New Products

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Products">
  Navigate to the Products section and click the **+** icon.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter Basic Information">
  Fill in the essential product details:
  - Product name
  - Product code or SKU
  - Description
  - Category
  - Product group (when using product groups)
</Step>

<Step title="Set Pricing">
  Configure the product pricing:
  - Base price
  - Price band (if using multiple price tiers)
  - Currency
</Step>

<Step title="Add Additional Information">
  Optionally add:
  - Product images
  - Allergen information
  - Nutritional data
  - Product tags
  - Notes or special instructions
</Step>

<Step title="Configure Availability">
  Set where and when the product is available:
  - Which sites or outlets can sell it
  - Time restrictions (if any)
  - User permission requirements
</Step>

<Step title="Save Product">
  Review all information and save the product to your catalog.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Product Pricing

### Setting Base Prices

Each product has a base price that serves as the default. To set or update a base price:

1. Go to the product details page
2. Click "Edit" or navigate to the Pricing section
3. Enter the new base price
4. Save your changes

### Price Bands

Price bands allow you to offer different prices for the same product:

- **Standard Price**: Regular price for most customers
- **Premium Price**: Higher price tier
- **Discount Price**: Lower price for promotions or specific customer segments

<Tip>
Use price bands to create different pricing strategies for the same product across different customer segments or locations.
</Tip>

### Price Schedules

You can schedule price changes to take effect at specific times:

- **Happy Hour Pricing**: Lower prices during specific hours
- **Seasonal Pricing**: Different prices for different seasons
- **Promotional Pricing**: Temporary price reductions

To create a price schedule:

1. Go to the product's pricing section
2. Click the **+** icon to add a price schedule
3. Set the start and end dates/times
4. Enter the price for that period
5. Save the schedule

### Location-Specific Pricing

You can set different prices for the same product at different locations:

1. Navigate to the product's pricing section
2. Click "Location Pricing" or "Site Pricing"
3. Select the location
4. Enter the price for that location
5. Save the pricing override

## Organizing Products with Groups

Product groups help you organize related products:

### Creating Product Groups

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Product Groups">
  Navigate to Products, then select "Product Groups" from the menu.
</Step>

<Step title="Create New Group">
  Click the **+** icon and enter:
  - Group name
  - Description
  - Group settings
</Step>

<Step title="Add Products">
  Assign products to the group by:
  - Selecting products individually
  - Using bulk selection
  - Importing from a file
</Step>

<Step title="Configure Group Settings">
  Set group-level configurations that apply to all products in the group:
  - Default pricing rules
  - Display settings
  - Availability rules
</Step>
</Steps>

### Benefits of Product Groups

- **Easier Management**: Update multiple products at once
- **Better Organization**: Group related products together
- **Simplified Reporting**: Generate reports by product group
- **Consistent Pricing**: Apply pricing rules across the group

## Product Restrictions

Control when and where products are available:

### Location Restrictions

Limit products to specific locations:

1. Go to the product's restrictions section
2. Select "Location Restrictions"
3. Choose which sites or outlets can sell the product
4. Save the restrictions

### Time Restrictions

Make products available only during certain hours:

1. Navigate to product restrictions
2. Select "Time Restrictions"
3. Set the days and times when the product is available
4. Configure different schedules for each location as required

### User Restrictions

Restrict products based on user roles or permissions:

- **Operator Roles**: Only certain operator roles can sell the product
- **User Permissions**: Require specific permissions to access the product
- **Age Restrictions**: Restrict products based on customer age (for age-restricted items)

## Product Sheets

Product sheets are used to display products on terminals and ordering systems:

### Creating Product Sheets

1. Go to Products, then select "Product Sheets"
2. Click the **+** icon to create a new sheet
3. Name your product sheet
4. Add products to the sheet
5. Configure display settings
6. Save the sheet

### Using Product Sheets

Product sheets can be:

- Assigned to specific terminals
- Used for ordering systems
- Displayed on kiosks
- Used for menu displays

## Product Insights

The Product Insights feature provides analytics on your products:

- **Sales Performance**: See which products sell best
- **Revenue Analysis**: Understand which products generate the most revenue
- **Trend Analysis**: Track product performance over time
- **Inventory Turnover**: See how quickly products move

Access Product Insights from the Products menu to view detailed analytics and reports.
