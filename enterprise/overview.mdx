## What is the Management Portal?

The Kappture Management Portal is your central hub for managing all aspects of your business operations. Whether you're tracking inventory, managing products, configuring sites and outlets, or analysing performance, the Management Portal provides the tools you need in one place.

## What You Can Do

The Management Portal helps you:

- **Manage Inventory**: Keep track of stock levels across all your locations, record deliveries, and perform stock takes
- **Control Products**: Maintain your product catalog, set prices, and configure when and where products are available
- **Organize Operations**: Set up and manage multiple sites, outlets, and preparation points
- **Monitor Performance**: View real-time dashboards and generate detailed reports
- **Control Access**: Manage who can access different parts of the system and what they can do

## Navigation Overview

The Management Portal is organized into clear sections accessible from the main navigation menu. The menu items you see depend on your user permissions.

### Top 5 Most Common Sections

1. **Analytics**: Access dashboards, reports, and activity tracking
2. **Stock**: Manage inventory, stock takes, deliveries, transfers, and adjustments
3. **Configuration > Management**: Configure products, layouts, tenders, sessions, operators, and more
4. **Configuration > Terminal**: Manage terminals, view status, and monitor session activity
5. **Reports**: Generate and view comprehensive business reports
