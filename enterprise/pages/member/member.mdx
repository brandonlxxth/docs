## Overview

The Member page lets you manage individual member records. Members are customers or users who can make purchases, have accounts, participate in loyalty programs, and more. You can create, edit, and manage member information and view their accounts, order history, and statements.

## Viewing Members

When you open the Member page, you'll see a **search box at the top**. Type your search (e.g., name, email, or member number) and results will load in a modal below. Each result shows member name, email, member number, status, associated accounts, and other key information.

## Creating a Member

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the Member page">
  Navigate to **Configuration > Member > Member** in the navigation menu.
</Step>

<Step title="Create new member">
  Click the **+** icon to create a new member.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter member information">
  Fill in the member details:
  - **Name**: Member's full name
  - **Email**: Email address (must be unique)
  - **Phone**: Phone number (optional)
  - **Member Number**: Unique identifier (auto-generated when left blank)
  - **Password**: Set initial password for member login
  - **Photo**: Upload a member photo (optional)
  - **Additional fields**: Any other configured member fields
</Step>

<Step title="Save the member">
  Review all information and click "Save" to create the member.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a Member

To edit an existing member:

1. Use the search box to find the member, then select it from the results
2. Click on the member or use the "Edit" action
3. Modify the member details as needed
4. Click "Save" to update the member

## Copying a Member

You can create a copy of an existing member record:

1. Use the search box to find the member, then select it
2. Click the "Copy" action
3. A new member form will open with the same details
4. Modify the name, email, and member number (these must be unique)
5. Update any other details as needed
6. Click "Save" to create the new member

## Member Accounts

### Viewing Member Accounts

To view all accounts associated with a member:

1. Open the member's details
2. Navigate to the "Accounts" tab or section
3. You'll see all accounts for that member, including:
   - Account name and type
   - Current balance
   - Account status

### Managing Member Accounts

From the member's account view, you can:

- View account details
- See transaction history
- Process top-ups or refunds when you have permissions
- Navigate to the full Account page for detailed management

## Member Order History

View a member's purchase history:

1. Open the member's details
2. Navigate to the "Order History" tab or section
3. You'll see all orders and purchases made by the member, including:
   - Order date and time
   - Items purchased
   - Total amount
   - Payment method
   - Location where order was placed

Use this information to:
- Understand member purchasing patterns
- Assist with customer service inquiries
- Process returns or refunds
- Analyze member behavior

## Member Statements

View financial statements for a member:

1. Open the member's details
2. Navigate to the "Statement" tab or section
3. You'll see a statement showing:
   - Account balances
   - Transactions (credits and debits)
   - Date range
   - Summary totals

Statements are useful for:
- Resolving billing questions
- Providing account summaries to members
- Financial reconciliation
- Audit purposes

## Changing Member Password

To change a member's password:

<Steps>
<Step title="Open member details">
  Find and open the member whose password you want to change.
</Step>

<Step title="Open change password dialog">
  Click the "Change Password" action or button.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter new password">
  In the change password dialog:
  - Enter the new password
  - Confirm the new password
  - Follow any password requirements (length, complexity, etc.)
</Step>

<Step title="Save password">
  Click "Save" or "Update Password" to change the password.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Only change member passwords when necessary and with proper authorization. Members should ideally change their own passwords through the member portal.
</Warning>

## Member Photos

### Uploading a Photo

To add or update a member's photo:

1. Open the member's details
2. Click on the photo area or "Upload Photo" button
3. Select an image file from your computer
4. The photo will be uploaded and associated with the member

### Viewing Photos

Member photos appear:
- In the search results (thumbnail)
- On the member's detail page
- On member cards or ID cards if printed
- In member search results

## Member QR Codes

Members can have QR codes generated for their accounts:

1. Open the member's details
2. The system can generate a QR code based on the member's token
3. QR codes can be used for:
   - Quick member identification
   - Mobile app login
   - Account access
   - Loyalty program scanning

<Info>
Contact your administrator for details about QR code features.
</Info>

## Fingerprint Management

Some systems support fingerprint identification for members:

1. Open the member's details
2. Navigate to the "Fingerprint" section
3. You can:
   - Enroll a fingerprint for the member
   - View fingerprint enrollment status
   - Manage fingerprint data

<Info>
Fingerprint features require compatible hardware and are not available in all configurations. Contact your administrator to determine if fingerprint features are enabled.
</Info>

## Validating Member Information

The system can validate certain member information:

### Email Validation

- Checks if an email address is already in use
- Validates email format
- Ensures uniqueness

### Token Validation

- Validates member tokens for QR codes or API access
- Checks token availability
- Ensures token uniqueness

## Deleting a Member

<Warning>
Deleting a member is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure the member has no active accounts, outstanding balances, or important transaction history before deleting.
</Warning>

To delete a member:

1. Use the search box to find the member, then select it
2. Click the "Delete" action
3. Confirm the deletion
4. The member will be permanently removed from the system

## Searching for Members

Use the **search box at the top** to find members. Type name, email, or member number and results load in the modal below. You can then sort or browse within the results as needed.

## Best Practices

- **Keep information current**: Regularly update member information as it changes
- **Verify email addresses**: Ensure email addresses are correct for communications
- **Use member groups**: Organize members into groups for targeted campaigns
- **Document changes**: Note any important changes or interactions with members
- **Respect privacy**: Only access member information when necessary and authorized

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Can't find a member">
  If you can't find a member:
  - Try different search terms (name, email, member number)
  - Try different search terms (name, email, member number)
  - Verify the member wasn't deleted
  - Contact your administrator if the member should exist
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Email already in use error">
  If you get an email already in use error:
  - Search for the existing member with that email
  - Update the existing member instead of creating a new one
  - Or use a different email address for the new member
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Member account balance seems wrong">
  If an account balance seems incorrect:
  - Check the member's statement for all transactions
  - Review order history for recent purchases
  - Verify account transactions in the Account section
  - Contact support if discrepancies persist
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't change member password">
  If password change fails:
  - Verify you have permission to change passwords
  - Check that the new password meets requirements
  - Ensure both password fields match
  - Try again or contact support
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
