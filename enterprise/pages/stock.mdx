## Overview

The Stock section provides comprehensive inventory management tools for tracking, managing, and reporting on your inventory across all locations.

<Warning>
**Requires Stock Module**: The entire Stock section requires the Stock module, which is charged separately. If you don't see this section in your navigation, contact your administrator or Kappture support to enable the Stock module.
</Warning>

## Main Pages

### Stock Alerts

View stock movement alerts and notifications. Get notified when stock levels are low, when movements occur, or when attention is needed.

## Stock Administration

Configuration and setup tools for stock management:

- **Unit of Measure**: Configure units of measure (kg, liters, units, etc.) for stock items
- **Supplier**: Manage supplier information and details
- **Order Prefix**: Configure order prefix codes for organizing stock orders
- **Reason Codes**: Manage stock reason codes for categorizing stock movements and adjustments
- **Product Price History**: View historical product price changes
- **Count Sheet**: Manage stock count sheets used during stock takes
- **Count Sheet Group**: Organize count sheets into groups for easier management
- **Par Level**: Configure par levels (target stock levels) for stock items
- **Product Waste**: Track and manage product waste with reasons and quantities
- **Product Depletion Group**: Configure product depletion groups for tracking ingredient usage
- **Product Requisition Group**: Configure product requisition groups for organizing requisitions
- **Product Insight**: View product insights and analytics related to stock
- **Stock Validation**: Validate stock data and identify discrepancies

## Stock Take

Tools for performing stock counts and verification:

- **Stock Take**: Perform stock counts and enter counted quantities
- **Stock Take Period**: Configure stock take periods for scheduling regular counts
- **Stock Take Period Assignment**: Assign stock take periods to specific outlets
- **Bulk Period Assignment**: Bulk assign stock take periods to multiple outlets at once
- **Stock Verification**: Verify stock counts and reconcile discrepancies
- **Stock History**: View stock history and historical stock levels

## Stock Dashboard

View a real-time stock dashboard with key metrics, including:
- Current stock levels
- Low stock alerts
- Recent movements
- Stock value
- Performance indicators

## Stock Movements

Manage all types of stock movements:

### Orders

- **Orders**: Create and manage stock orders to suppliers
- **Order Status**: View order status and track order progress

### Deliveries

- **Deliveries**: Record and manage stock deliveries from suppliers
- **Delivery Report**: View delivery reports and history

### Return

- **Returns**: Process stock returns to suppliers
- **Return Report**: View return reports and history

### Transfer

- **Transfer**: Transfer stock between locations, outlets, or preparation points
- **Transfer Template**: Create reusable transfer templates for common transfers
- **Bulk Transfer**: Perform bulk stock transfers for multiple items at once
- **Replenish Stock**: Replenish stock at locations from central inventory
- **Withdraw Stock**: Withdraw stock from locations
- **Transfer Report**: View transfer reports and history

### Adjustments

- **Adjustment**: Make stock adjustments to correct inventory discrepancies
- **Adjustment Report**: View adjustment reports and history

### Requisition

- **Requisition**: Create stock requisitions to request stock from other locations
- **Outlet to Outlet Requisition**: Create requisitions between outlets
- **Requisition Report**: View requisition reports and fulfillment status
