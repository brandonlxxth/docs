## Overview

The Cash Management section provides tools for handling financial operations, cash management, and revenue tracking.

<Warning>
**Requires Revenue Module**: This section requires the Revenue module, which is charged separately. If you don't see this section in your navigation, contact your administrator or [Kappture Sales](/contact) to enable the Revenue module.
</Warning>

## Required permissions

Cash Management permissions are configured in **Admin > User Roles** under the **Cash Management** tab.

| Feature | User Role Permission | Tab in User Role Config |
|---------|---------------------|------------------------|
| Cash Declaration | Cash Declaration | Cash Management |
| Cash Events | Cash Events | Cash Management |
| Drawer Totals | Drawer Totals | Cash Management |
| Outlet Hourly Sales | Outlet Hourly Sales | Cash Management |
| Quick Bank | Quick Bank | Cash Management |
| Quick Receive | Quick Receive | Cash Management |
| Received Bank Summary | Received Bank Summary | Cash Management |
| Revenue Tracker | Revenue Tracker | Cash Management |
| Reason Code | Reason Code | Cash Management |
| Cash Vault | Cash Vault | Cash Management |
| Terminal Session Cash | Terminal Session Cash | Cash Management |

## Features

The Cash Management section includes tools for:

- Revenue tracking and reporting
- Cash handling and reconciliation
- Financial operations management
- Transaction monitoring

<Info>
Contact your administrator for the list of pages and features available in the Revenue module.
</Info>
