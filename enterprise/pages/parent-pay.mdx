## Overview

The Parent Pay section provides features for managing parent payment accounts and transactions.

<Warning>
**Requires Parent Pay Module**: This section requires the Parent Pay module, which is charged separately. If you don't see this section in your navigation, contact your administrator or Kappture support to enable the Parent Pay module.
</Warning>

## Features

The Parent Pay section includes tools for:

- Parent account management
- Payment processing
- Account balance tracking
- Transaction history
- Payment settings and configuration

<Info>
Contact your administrator for the list of pages and features available in the Parent Pay module.
</Info>
