## Overview

**Cash Vault** is used to record and view **terminal cash vault** declarations â€” the cash stored in the vault at a terminal (e.g. a safe or locked drawer). You can **create** a new declaration (terminal, value, and references) and **view** historical declarations with filters by date, outlet, and user. The view shows whether each declaration has been **Processed**, so you can track vault cash for reconciliation and auditing.

## Required permissions

| Feature | User Role Permission | Tab in User Role Config |
|---------|---------------------|------------------------|
| Cash Vault | Cash Vault | Cash Management |

## Accessing Cash Vault

Navigate to **Cash Management > Cash Vault**. The main page is the **view** (list of declarations with filters). Use the **Add** (plus) button in the card toolbar to open the **create declaration** form.

## Creating a cash vault declaration

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Cash Vault">
  Go to **Cash Management > Cash Vault**.
</Step>

<Step title="Start new declaration">
  Click the **Add** (plus) button in the card toolbar.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter details">
  - **Terminal**: Select the terminal where the cash vault declaration is being made.
  - **Value**: Enter the monetary value being declared for the terminal cash vault (the amount stored in the vault at that moment).
  - **Reference 1**: Primary reference (e.g. bag number or unique reference code). Recommended for tracking.
  - **Reference 2** (optional): Secondary reference.
  - **Reference 3** (optional): Tertiary reference.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Click the **green tick** to save. The declaration appears in the vault list and can be filtered and reviewed in the view.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The **Value** must reflect the accurate amount in the vault at the time of declaration. Use at least one reference so the declaration can be matched to operational or banking documentation.
</Note>

## Viewing cash vault declarations

The main Cash Vault page shows a table of declarations. Use the filters to narrow the list:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date** | Select a single date or date range. Only declarations within that range are shown. |
| **Outlet** | Restrict to declarations for terminals in the selected outlet. |
| **User** | Restrict to declarations created by the selected operator. |

### Table columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Terminal** | Terminal where the declaration was recorded. |
| **Date** | Date and time of the declaration. |
| **User** | Operator who made the declaration. |
| **Value** | Declared cash amount. |
| **Reference** | Primary reference (e.g. Reference 1). |
| **Processed** | Whether the declaration has been processed (for your workflow or integration). |
| **Actions** | View or manage the record as provided by the UI. |

Use the view to audit vault declarations, verify values and references, and check processed status.
