## Overview

**Outlet Hourly Sales** shows sales data broken down **by hour** for each **outlet**. Use it to see when sales peak during the day, to compare outlets by time of day, and to support staffing or operational decisions. The page loads a table or grid of hourly sales (and possibly totals) per outlet; the exact columns and metrics depend on your configuration.

## Required permissions

| Feature | User Role Permission | Tab in User Role Config |
|---------|---------------------|------------------------|
| Outlet Hourly Sales | Outlet Hourly Sales | Cash Management |

## Accessing Outlet Hourly Sales

Navigate to **Cash Management > Revenue Tracker > Outlet Hourly Sales**.

## Using the page

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the page">
  Go to **Cash Management > Revenue Tracker > Outlet Hourly Sales**. The page loads and may default to today or a recent period.
</Step>

<Step title="Review the table">
  The main area shows a table (or similar) with outlets as rows and hours as columns (or the reverse). Each cell typically shows the sales value or count for that outlet in that hour. Use it to see patterns (e.g. lunch peak, quiet hours).
</Step>

<Step title="Change period or filters if available">
  If the page offers a date selector or outlet filter, use it to change the period or restrict to specific outlets.
</Step>
</Steps>

## What the page shows

- **Outlet** — Each row (or column) usually represents one outlet.
- **Hours** — Time slots (e.g. 09:00, 10:00, …) so you can see sales per hour.
- **Values** — Sales amount or quantity for each outlet/hour combination.
- **Totals** — Often a total row or column for the day or period.

Outlet Hourly Sales is **read-only** and is for reporting and analysis only.
