## Overview

The Fiscal section provides fiscal reporting and compliance features for organizations that require detailed fiscal documentation and reporting.

<Warning>
**Requires Fiscal Module**: This section requires fiscal features to be enabled for your organization and appropriate permissions. If you don't see this section in your navigation, contact your administrator or Kappture support.
</Warning>

## Features

The Fiscal section includes tools for:

- Fiscal reporting and documentation
- Compliance reporting
- Tax reporting and summaries
- Fiscal printer integration
- Regulatory compliance

<Info>
The specific pages available in this section depend on your organization's configuration, location, and the fiscal requirements for your region. Contact your administrator for details about available fiscal features.
</Info>
