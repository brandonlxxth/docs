## Overview

The Fiscal section provides fiscal reporting and compliance features for organisations that require detailed fiscal documentation and reporting.

<Warning>
**Requires Fiscal Module**: This section requires fiscal features to be enabled for your organization and appropriate permissions. If you don't see this section in your navigation, contact your administrator or [Kappture Sales](/contact).
</Warning>

## Required permissions

Fiscal permissions are configured in **Admin > User Roles** under the **Fiscal** tab (only visible for France NF525).

| Feature | User Role Permission | Tab in User Role Config |
|---------|---------------------|------------------------|
| Configuration | Configuration | Fiscal |
| Reports | Reports | Fiscal |
| Tax Archive | Tax Archive | Fiscal |
| Signing Key | Signing Key | Fiscal |
| Period Closing | Period Closing | Fiscal |

## Features

The Fiscal section includes tools for:

- Fiscal reporting and documentation
- Compliance reporting
- Tax reporting and summaries
- Fiscal printer integration
- Regulatory compliance

<Info>
Contact your administrator for the list of fiscal pages and features available for your region.
</Info>
