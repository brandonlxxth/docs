## Need help?

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions about the Kappture platform, technical issues, or feature requests.

## Common support requests

| Request | What to include |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Report issues** | Report name, date range, filters used, and error message (if any) |
| **Module access** | Module name you need enabled and your organisation details |
| **Day threshold adjustments** | Which reports are timing out and your typical date range requirements |
| **Feature requests** | Description of the feature and how it would help your workflow |
| **Bug reports** | Steps to reproduce, expected behaviour, actual behaviour, and screenshots |

## Before contacting support

<Steps>
<Step title="Check the documentation">
  Many common questions are answered in our documentation. Use the search function to find relevant articles.
</Step>

<Step title="Note your organisation details">
  Have your organisation name ready so we can quickly locate your account.
</Step>

<Step title="Gather relevant information">
  For technical issues, note any error messages, the steps that led to the issue, and which browser you're using.
</Step>
</Steps>
